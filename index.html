Your Safety First

Safety & Guidelines

Your safety is our top priority. All rafting activities in Kithulgala are conducted under strict international safety standards with trained guides and certified equipment.

Certified Equipment

All helmets, life jackets, paddles, and rafts are internationally certified and inspected daily before every expedition.

Professional Guides

Our rafting guides are professionally trained, river-certified, and experienced in handling all rapid levels safely.

Pre-Rafting Briefing

A full safety briefing and paddling demonstration is provided before entering the river to ensure everyone is confident and prepared.

Age & Health Requirements

Participants should be in good health. Minimum age and rafting level requirements depend on river conditions and water levels.

Weather & River Conditions

Rafting schedules may change depending on rainfall and river flow to ensure maximum safety at all times.

Emergency Support

First aid kits, rescue equipment, and emergency response procedures are always in place throughout the rafting route.
