Adventure Awaits

Kithulgala River Adventures

Experience thrilling outdoor adventures in the heart of Sri Lanka. From white water rafting on the Kelani River to jungle canyoning and waterfall abseiling, Kithulgala offers unforgettable moments surrounded by lush rainforests and flowing rivers.

White Water Rafting

Paddle through exciting rapids on the Kelani River with professional guides and full safety equipment. Perfect for beginners and adventure lovers.

Canyoning

Explore natural rock formations, jungle streams, and hidden pools while climbing, sliding, and jumping through scenic canyons.

Zip Lining

Fly above the river and rainforest on an adrenaline-filled zip line experience with breathtaking aerial views of Kithulgala.

Waterfall Abseiling

Descend stunning waterfalls using professional climbing equipment under expert supervision for a thrilling vertical adventure.

Kayaking

Enjoy calm stretches and gentle rapids while kayaking through the scenic river landscapes of Kithulgala.

Camping & Day Out

Relax under the stars with riverside camping and day-out packages that combine adventure, nature, and local cuisine.
